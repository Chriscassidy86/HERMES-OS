# V5 Continuous PAPER Platform

V5.1 connects continuous market cycles to the existing session, Risk Manager, portfolio, and journal. V5.2 adds explainable BUY/SELL/HOLD/WAIT outcomes. V5.3 adds live GET-only dashboard refresh. V5.4 learns from completed PAPER trades without mutation. V5.5 validates accelerated 24-hour bounded operation. V5.6 hardens and audits the release.

No V5 component authenticates to an exchange or places a real order.
