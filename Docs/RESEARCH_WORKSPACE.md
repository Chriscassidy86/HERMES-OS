# Research Workspace

Research jobs are immutable, content-identified, row-bounded, PAPER-only definitions over cataloged fixture, replay, public-observation, or paper data. Submission revalidates dataset identifiers, configuration labels, and resource bounds. Completed manifests persist through the research repository; comparison and reproducibility export remain offline.

Cancellation is safe while queued. Execution is currently synchronous, so a running job is not advertised as cancellable. No result is automatically adopted and no strategy, specialist weight, or risk rule is mutated.
