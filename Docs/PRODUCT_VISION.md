# Product Vision

Hermes OS is the operating system for explainable quantitative research at Hermes
Quant Labs. It combines validated public observations, independent specialists,
weighted evidence, deterministic risk, paper accounting, audit storage, governed
experiments, and executive reporting.

The product advances from fixtures to historical replay, read-only public
observation, and durable paper operation. Live trading is not a V2 goal. Success
means trustworthy decisions, measurable learning, simple recovery, honest reports,
and low operating cost—not guaranteed returns.
