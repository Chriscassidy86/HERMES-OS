# Hermes OS V3 Roadmap

V3 evolves Hermes OS into an explainable, PAPER-only quantitative research
platform without replacing the deterministic V2 safety and accounting core.

1. V3.1: immutable human-readable decision explanations.
2. V3.2: validated ten-state market regime research engine.
3. V3.3: deterministic multi-timeframe specialist intelligence.
4. V3.4: read-only CEO dashboard projection and renderer.
5. V3.5: causal post-trade learning explanations.
6. V3.6: versioned research provenance, reproducibility, evaluation, and local delivery.

Future V3 work should deepen offline research quality, provenance review, and
artifact navigation. Live trading, authenticated exchange clients, automatic
configuration adoption, and Risk Manager bypass remain outside this release line.
