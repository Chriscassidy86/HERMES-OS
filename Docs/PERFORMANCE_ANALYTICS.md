# Performance Analytics

Advanced analytics validate finite inputs and explicit UTC timestamps, then calculate realized/unrealized P&L, returns, trade statistics, drawdown, costs, exposure, turnover, calibration, decision quality, and deterministic groupings. Annualized return requires at least 365 days. Sharpe-style and Sortino-style values disclose their zero-target, no-risk-free-rate assumptions and are withheld when variance or samples are insufficient.

Fixture, replay, public-observation, and PAPER results remain separated. Reports make no profitability claim.

V6 projections add UTC equity, cash, and exposure curves, latest-symbol concentration, average holding duration, and pairwise correlation only when both series have enough non-zero variance.
